Basic Facts about Fake News

Fake news is false or misleading information presented as news. It often aims to deceive and manipulate readers for various purposes, including political propaganda, financial gain, or sensationalism.

Here are some key points to understand about fake news:

  • Fake news can spread rapidly through social media platforms, reaching a wide audience within minutes.
  • It is essential to verify information from multiple reliable sources before believing or sharing it.
  • Media literacy and critical thinking skills are crucial for discerning between credible news sources and fake news.
  • Understanding the motives behind fake news can help individuals recognize and avoid falling victim to misinformation.
