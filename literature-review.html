Literature Review

Research studies on fake news have revealed several interesting findings:

  1. A study published in the Journal of Communication found that exposure to fake news can significantly influence people's perceptions and beliefs.
  2. Research conducted by the Pew Research Center indicates that social media users are more likely to encounter fake news stories than traditional news sources.
